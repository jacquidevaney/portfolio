WSJ. Magazine
Redesign

Overview

WSJ. Magazine runs monthly with the weekend edition of The Wall Street Journal. The magazine features beautiful design that reflects a well crafted and luxurious reading experience. I worked with the print design and editorial team to creat designs that reflected the WSJ. Magazine brand.

Tools Used

I used pen and paper for inital mockups and created designs in illustrator. I coded the templates for the WSJ iPad app using HTML, javascript and .json.
