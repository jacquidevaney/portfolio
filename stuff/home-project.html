Multimedia Journalism Project:
What Is Home?

Overview

Headed by Josh Davis, we (Studio 20) started this project with a question: what is home? For the first part of the project, I filmed and edited a video about a park ranger at Bear Mountain State Park, RJ, whose ancestry traces back to the park in the 1800s. Once all of the videos were shot and ready, I assumed the role of Art Director. I styled the video posterframes and was responsible for the look and feel of the entire site.
See the full project here.

Tools Used

For the video, I used a Canon t2i and t4i, a 50mm lens and a 18-55mm lens. For the audio, I used a Zoom and a shotgun microphone. For the time lapse, I used an external intervalometer. For the website, I coded the CSS and created the video posterframes in Illustrator.

Awards

This project won the Award of Excellence at CPOY in 2015.
