  • The Wall Street Journal - Graduate Thesis Project
  • September 2015 - December 2015
  • My thesis project centers around creating context and background for the news using a tool I created named Accordion. see more +
    What I've found is that many readers can get lost in the complex, ever evolving news cycle and gaining valuable background is arduous, from having to go back and read a laundry list of old articles to googling until you give up. I hypothesized that there is a way for news companies to give the value of background to a user right in an article. And thus, Accordion was born. It's an in-line tool that expands and collapses with extra info for readers to digest while reading a story. The idea is that the background is there when they need it, without having to leave the page or search on their own.
